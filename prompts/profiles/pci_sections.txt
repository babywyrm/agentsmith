PCI-DSS v4.0 — Payment card data protection:

Req 3: Protect stored account data — encryption, hashing, truncation, never store CVV
Req 4: Strong crypto in transit (TLS 1.2+)
Req 6: Secure development, vuln management, code reviews
Req 7: Restrict access to cardholder data by need-to-know
Req 8: Strong authentication, MFA
Req 10: Log and monitor access to cardholder data
Key Management: Secure storage, rotation, access controls

Focus on: Payment processing, checkout, tokenization, PAN/CVV handling, key storage.
